About Moldova

Moldova is a country located in Eastern Europe, bordered by Romania and Ukraine. It has a population of about 3.5 million people and a rich cultural heritage.

Geography
Interesting Facts
Capital City

The capital of Moldova is Chișinău, which is the cultural, political, and economic center of the country.

The official language of Moldova is Romanian, but Russian is also widely spoken.

The currency used in Moldova is the Moldovan Leu (MDL).

Tourist Attractions

Orheiul Vechi

Orheiul Vechi is a historical and archaeological complex in Moldova, located in Trebujeni, about 60 kilometers (37 miles) northeast of Chișinău on the Răut River in the Republic of Moldova.

Chișinău

Chișinău, formerly known as Kishinev, is the capital and largest city of the Republic of Moldova. It is the main industrial and commercial center of Moldova, located in the center of the country, on the Bîc River, a tributary of the Dniester.

Călărași

A beautiful town in northern Moldova, known for its natural beauty and outdoor activities. Călărași (Romanian pronunciation: [kələˈraʃʲ]) is a town in Moldova, founded in 1848.

Tourist Destination Gallery in Moldova

Cricova Winery

Cricova, near Chișinău, is a famous winery known for its extensive wine cellars and high-quality wines. Tours include wine tasting sessions and visits to the wine storage tunnels.

Zity Mall

Zity Mall is a shopping center in Chișinău offering various stores, restaurants, and entertainment, providing a modern shopping experience for visitors.

Piata Centrala

Chișinău's central market is a bustling place where locals and visitors can find fresh produce, local products, and various goods. It's an ideal spot to experience local culture and cuisine.

Asconi Winery

Located in the Stefan Voda district, Asconi Winery is renowned for its wine production and beautiful vineyards. It offers wine tours and tasting sessions to showcase Moldova's winemaking heritage.

Botanical Garden of the Academy of Sciences of Moldova

This expansive garden in Chișinău features a collection of plants from around the world. It is a great place for relaxation and botanical study.

OrheiLand

OrheiLand is the largest amusement park in Moldova, offering a variety of rides and attractions for all ages, making it a favorite destination for families.

Tank Monument

This monument in Chișinău commemorates Soviet tanks from World War II. It reflects the wartime history of Moldova and the significance of these events.

Tiraspol

Tiraspol is the administrative center of Transnistria, known for its Soviet architecture and unique political status. Tiraspol offers a different perspective on Moldova's history.

Suvorov Monument

This monument in Chișinău honors Russian military leader Alexander Suvorov. The historical site serves as an important symbol in Moldova's military history.
